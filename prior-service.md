Characters cycle through this table during each term of service.
The reenlistment die throw is required even if the character does not intend to reenlist (a role of 12 exactly calls for mandatory reenlistment).
DM's are cumulative (in the case of enlistment) if the characters have the necessary prerequisities.
All roles except draft are two-die (2D6) rolls.
